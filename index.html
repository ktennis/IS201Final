About

For 75 years the Capitol has controlled the Districts. They have turned us against eachother by making our children fight in the Hunger Games. Tonight, let us turn our weapons away from eachother and against the Capitol. Let us join the Mockingjay in the fight for justice!

Who are we?

We are District 13. We survived the bombings from the capitol 75 years ago and have been living underground, waiting for an opportunity to rid the world of the Capitol.

  • Leader: Alma Coin
  • Industry: Nuclear Weapons and Graphite Mining
  • Sq. Miles: 102,978
  • Population: 14,756
  • Location: North-East Panem

Who is the Mockingjay?

Our Mockingjay is Katniss Everdeen. Katniss comes from the seam of district 12. She is the survivor of 2 Hunger Games. She sparked the rebellion when she pulled out nightlock berries in her first hunger games. This resulted in both her and Peeta Mellark being crowned victors but was seen as an act of defiance by the Capitol.
